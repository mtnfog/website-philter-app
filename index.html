Identify and redact PHI and PII from text and documents

Philter redacts PII and PHI from documents to help you maintain HIPAA compliance, meet industry regulations, and leverage your documents for valuable secondary purposes.

Identify over 30 types of sensitive information for redaction, tokenization, masking, encryption, and anonymization.

Philter runs in your cloud

Philter runs in your cloud for optimal data security. Your data never leaves your cloud. It's your data - keep it away from third parties.

Whether its patient healthcare, legal, or other type of document, Philter can redact it without ever needing your data to leave your private cloud.

Keep PHI and PII out of your data processing pipelines

Philter can redact, anonymize, tokenize, and encrypt PHI and PII to help keep your data pipelines free of sensitive information and maintain your organization's HIPAA compliance.

Works with Apache NiFi to redact data in motion

As a Cloudera partner, Philter works with Cloudera DataFlow. Philter can redact sensitive information inside your Apache NiFi data flows.

Add-Ins for Redacting Microsoft Office documents

The Philter Add-Ins for Microsoft Office allow you to redact your Microsoft Word documents and Microsoft Excel spreadsheets.
