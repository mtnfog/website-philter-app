Redact PHI and PII from Microsoft Office documents

The Philter Add-Ins for Microsoft Office provide add-ins for Microsoft Word and Microsoft Excel to redact your documents.

The add-ins are available for free to Philter users. Contact us to get started.

Works with Microsoft Word and Microsoft Excel documents

The add-ins work with Philter to redact text in your Microsoft Word documents and Microsoft Excel spreadsheets. Redact your documents from right inside Microsoft Office.
